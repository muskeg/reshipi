# Crusty French Bread

<div class="recipe-hero" markdown>
<div class="recipe-intro" markdown>

Dutch oven bread ready in just a few hours — no overnight rise needed. Crispy crust on the outside and soft, airy bread on the inside.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Servings** | 6–8 |
| **Prep time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook time** | 45 minutes |

## Ingredients

- <span class="qty" data-qty="2.25" data-unit="tsp">2 ¼ tsp</span> active dry yeast (<span class="qty" data-qty="9" data-unit="g">9 g</span>)
- <span class="qty" data-qty="1" data-unit="tsp">1 tsp</span> sugar (<span class="qty" data-qty="4" data-unit="g" data-type="sugar">4 g</span>)
- <span class="qty" data-qty="1.25" data-unit="cup" data-type="water">1 ¼ cups</span> warm water (<span class="qty" data-qty="300" data-unit="g">300 g</span>)
- <span class="qty" data-qty="1.25" data-unit="tsp">1 ¼ tsp</span> kosher salt (<span class="qty" data-qty="8" data-unit="g" data-type="salt">8 g</span>)
- <span class="qty" data-qty="3" data-unit="cup" data-type="flour">3 cups</span> all-purpose flour (<span class="qty" data-qty="400" data-unit="g" data-type="flour">400 g</span>), plus extra for dusting

## Instructions

1. Combine yeast, sugar, and warm water in a large mixing bowl. Let proof for about 5 minutes until foamy.
2. Add flour, mix with a sturdy spatula until dough starts to come together, then add salt and mix until no dry bits remain. The dough will be shaggy and sticky.
3. Cover with a tea towel and let rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
4. Lightly flour a cutting board. Tip the dough out — do not punch down.
5. Shape into a round loaf: pull each corner toward the center and repeat until the dough feels tight. Flip over.
6. Place in a floured proofing basket or bowl seam-side down. Cover and let rise 30 minutes.
7. Meanwhile, place Dutch oven with lid in your oven and preheat to 460 °F (240 °C).
8. Tip bread onto parchment paper, seam side up. Carefully transfer to the hot Dutch oven.
9. Bake covered for 30 minutes. Remove lid and bake another 10–15 minutes until deep golden brown.
10. Remove bread and let cool on a wire rack for at least 30 minutes before slicing.
